Tutorial

Click on the Tiles to reveal them.

The number on the Tile means how many bombs there are around it (+diagonally).

To win, you have to mark ALL Bombs with Flags (right click).

You can Restart any time with Smile Button.

You can see the number of Bombs remained in the left corner and your time in the right one.

*Your Records are saved until you completely close the browser.
